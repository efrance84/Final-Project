Welcome!

Welcome to the Psych Private Detective Agency, where we provide all your detective needs. We are a detective agency founded by Shawn Spencer and Burton Guster, two best friends and partners.

Shawn is a psychic detective who assists police in solving cases. With his uncanny observational skills, he is able to uncover clues that no one else can. Burton is Shawn’s best friend and sidekick, and together they work to solve cases and help people in need.

Our agency is dedicated to providing you with the best detective services in town. We offer a variety of services, from private investigations to criminal defense. Whatever your needs may be, we are here to help.

Our team consists of Shawn Spencer and Burton Guster.
